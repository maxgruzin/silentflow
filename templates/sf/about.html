About Silent Flow

Silent Flow is a free digital music distribution platform.


All our releases are available for free, we do not commercially benefit from music in any way. Our only goal is to promote artists and music they create.
Artists hold the right to their works and can distribute or publish them by themselves. All releases are published under Creative Commons license.

Contact & Submission

Feel free to contact us by email. If you're willing to become a part of our label, please send a link to your material - use file transfer sites instead of attaching files to email.
